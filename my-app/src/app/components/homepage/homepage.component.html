What We Are

The Beast provides clients in the Department of Psychiatry at UNC-Chapel Hill a platform to answer surveys about their patients.


The clients for The Beast are various Employment Survey Providers.


These surveys include: Person Level Data Surveys (in development), Staff Level Data Surveys, Closed Surveys, IPS Logs, and Job Developments Logs.


The front end of this project is coded in Angular. The back end in Node.js, express.js, and JavaScript with MongoDB as the database.


We must manage our data in accordance with HIPAA.


On this website you can access any of the surveys, click on a survey to begin.
